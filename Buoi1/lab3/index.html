About Seattle University

Seattle University is a prestigious private university located in Seattle, Washington, USA. Founded in 1891, the university follows the Jesuit tradition, emphasizing critical thinking, ethics, and social responsibility. Seattle University offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs in fields such as business, engineering, law, social sciences, and healthcare. Situated in the heart of Seattle—one of the most innovative and fastest-growing cities in the U.S.—the university provides students with numerous opportunities for internships and careers at major corporations like Microsoft, Amazon, and Boeing.
